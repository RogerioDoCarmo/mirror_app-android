# Privacy Policy

**Miroji · Last updated: 14 June 2026**

> **Short version:** Miroji is a simple mirror. It shows your device's front-camera feed on screen in real time. It does **not** collect, store, record, or transmit any personal data, photos, or video. Everything happens on your device.

The hosted version of this policy (the URL used in the Google Play and App Store listings) is published at:
**https://rogeriodocarmo.github.io/mirror_app/privacy-policy.html**

## 1. Who we are

Miroji ("the app", "we", "us") is a mobile application that uses your device's front-facing camera to display a live mirror view. This policy explains how the app handles information.

## 2. Information we collect

**None.** Miroji does not collect, request, or store any personal or sensitive user data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs that gather data.

## 3. Camera usage

Miroji requires camera permission for a single purpose: to display your front-camera feed on the screen as a mirror. The camera feed is processed live, on your device, and is shown only to you. The app does **not** take photos, does **not** record video, does **not** save any frames, and does **not** send the camera feed anywhere. Nothing from the camera ever leaves your device.

## 4. Device language

The app reads your device's language setting locally to display its interface in a supported language (English, Portuguese, Spanish, or Japanese). This preference is read on-device only and is never collected or transmitted.

## 5. Data sharing and selling

Because Miroji collects no data, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose to third parties.

## 6. Data retention and security

Miroji stores no personal data, so there is no user data to retain, delete, or secure on any server. The app operates entirely on your device.

## 7. Children's privacy

Miroji does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app contains no ads, no purchases, and no data collection.

## 8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

## 9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact <contact@rogeriodocarmo.com>.
