Miroji is a fast, simple mirror for your phone's front camera.

Open it and you immediately see a live view from your front camera — just like looking into a mirror. No shutter, no saving, no sign-up.

Private by design: nothing is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. Miroji only asks for the camera permission, and the camera image never leaves your device.

Features:

* Instant live front-camera mirror
* No ads, no trackers, no analytics
* No account or sign-up required
* Nothing is recorded or saved
* Clean, minimal interface
* Available in English, Portuguese, Spanish and Japanese

Miroji is free and open source.
